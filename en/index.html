Flagship product

HumanOS

HumanOS is Sinapsis' flagship product: a personal and business operating system to organize tasks, calendar, finances, companies, data, decisions and AI assistants in a single environment.

What it solves
  • Information scattered across emails, spreadsheets, calendars and conversations.
  • Lack of follow-up on commitments and decisions.
  • Difficulty seeing companies, finances, tasks and projects as a whole.
  • Manual reports that consume time.
  • Operational overload for professionals, entrepreneurs and small teams.

Compliance

Security, privacy and governance by design

We design considering Chilean Law No. 21,719 (Personal Data Protection) and Law No. 21,663 (Cybersecurity Framework), with international standards as reference.

Information security

  • We design systems considering access control, least privilege, traceability, backups, environment separation and incident management.

Privacy and data protection

  • We process data under criteria of purpose, minimization, proportionality, security and transparency.

AI governance

  • We use AI with human oversight, risk assessment, reasonable traceability and documented use cases.

Operational continuity

  • We design solutions with backup, recovery, monitoring and operational resilience in mind.
