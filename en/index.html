Trust and compliance

Security, privacy and compliance by design

We design our solutions considering current Chilean regulation and take international standards as our working reference.

Chilean regulation

  • Law No. 21,719 — Personal Data Protection: defined purposes, lawful bases, data subject rights and incident management.
  • Law No. 21,663 — Cybersecurity Framework Law: information security and operational continuity as design criteria.
  • Privacy, information security and AI governance integrated into the lifecycle of every solution — not bolted on at the end.

Reference standards

  • We prepare documentation and controls compatible with international frameworks for security, privacy, quality and risk management.
  • AI system governance with human oversight, traceability and risk assessment.
ISO/IEC 27001 ISO/IEC 27701 ISO/IEC 42001 ISO 22301 ISO 9001 ISO 31000 ISO/IEC 27035
